This folder contains all constraint files we have utilised when creating the 
SIPHER Synthetic Population. In addition, we have provided all underlying 
Rcode pipelines which we have used for creating these files. These files 
provide information on how we accessed, scraped, and recoded online resources 
such as NOMIS or NRS data.
